2kSports

COMPANY

2K Sports is a development studio that develops and publishes sports-based video games. Its product portfolio includes NBA 2K series, the NHL 2K series, the WWE 2K series, and MLB 2K series. 2K Sports is a division of 2K Games and was founded in January 2005. The company is headquartered in Novato, California.
